How to Apply

Step 1: Choose Your Program

Browse our list of vocational programs and select the one that best suits your career goals.

Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Complete the online application form with your personal and educational details.

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

Pay the application fee to complete your application process. Payment instructions will be sent to you after form submission.
